Play to Secure

A gamified cybersecurity education platform teaching secure coding through interactive attack simulations, themed levels, leaderboards, and a community wall.

  • 7 vulnerability modules: SQLi, IDOR, XSS, NoSQL, Clickjacking, DOM Clobbering, RBAC
  • Map-style level progression system
  • Distinct themed UI per attack (comic, jungle, chess, theatre)
  • Interactive challenges, quizzes & guided explanations
  • Game leaderboard + community contribution leaderboard
  • Social wall with posts, comments & reactions
  • Mystery gift rewards unlocked at milestones
  • Admin: quiz management, video library, user notifications
  • Behaviour-based targeted encouragement from admins

UrbanFix

A community safety & media platform enabling citizens to report emergencies, engage in local discussions, and coordinate civic action — with one-tap SOS alerts.

  • OTP-based authentication & NID verification badges
  • One-tap panic button with camera, mic & location sharing
  • Dedicated police, fire & ambulance quick-call buttons
  • Offline-capable crime & disaster reporting with media
  • Real-time nearby emergency feed with community response
  • Reddit-style boards: Reports, Events, Polls, Donations, Volunteer
  • Contributor leaderboard by area (daily/weekly/monthly)
  • Admin dashboard, content moderation & push announcements
  • Support ticketing system between users and admins

An AI-powered lost & found system with confidence-scored auto-matching, admin oversight, gamified rewards, and full post lifecycle management.

  • AI auto-matching by text, location & timestamp

  • Comments, replies, bookmarks & item claiming
