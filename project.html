Lost and Found Website

A Lost and Found platform with AI-powered auto-matching, user interaction, admin control, and gamified rewards.

  • AI-powered auto-matching based on text, location, and time
  • Create, edit, and delete lost/found item posts
  • Attach images, descriptions, titles, and locations
  • Comment, reply, and delete interactions on posts
  • Bookmark and claim items through the platform
  • Customizable user profiles with activity tracking
  • Admin panel for reviewing, deleting, and moderating posts
  • Automatic archiving of unresolved posts after 15 days
  • Gamification with points, badges, and leaderboard system
  • Confidence scoring and match notifications for admins and users
